TMS by Expendifii

Transport Management System designed for small and medium transport companies.

What is TMS?

TMS is a web-based system that helps transport companies manage their daily operations such as creating bookings, printing documents, and generating e-way bills.

Instead of maintaining manual registers or using outdated software, everything is managed from one simple website.

Main Problems We Solve

Manual GR Writing

Transport offices often write GR manually or maintain Excel sheets. This leads to errors and slow operations.

Document Printing

Generating loading slips, manifests, and other documents manually takes time.

E-way Bill Creation

Users currently have to login to the GST portal and fill many fields manually.
