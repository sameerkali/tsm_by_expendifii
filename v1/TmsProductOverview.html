How It Works

A day in the life of a TMS user — step by step

1
Morning — Check the Dashboard
The owner or manager opens TMS on their browser or phone. The dashboard shows: how many bookings were made today, how many trips are active, and how many deliveries are pending. One screen. Everything at a glance.
2
Create a Booking (GR)
When a consignment comes in, the booking clerk opens a new GR form. Customer name, vehicle, driver, product, weight, route — most of these are already saved in the system. The clerk selects from a dropdown, enters weight, and the freight amount calculates automatically. Done in under 2 minutes.
3
⚡ Generate E-Way Bill — The Big One
This is where TMS saves the most time. Once the GR is created, the clerk clicks one button: Generate E-Way Bill. The system automatically pulls all required details — company GSTIN, customer GSTIN, vehicle number, product HSN code, weight, declared value — and sends it directly to the government's e-way bill system through our licensed API connection. Within 20 to 60 seconds, the e-way bill number is back and saved on the booking. No government portal. No re-typing. No site crashes.
4
Print Documents
The clerk selects the booking, clicks Print, and a clean document comes out — with the company logo and letterhead already set up in settings. Filter by customer or date range and print multiple documents in one go.
5
Track and Update Status
As goods move, the status is updated: Booked → In Transit → Delivered. The dashboard reflects this in real time.
6
End of Month — Automatic Backup
TMS automatically emails the company a full data backup every month. All your bookings, customers, vehicle records — sent directly to your inbox in Excel format. You always have a copy of your own data.

Core Features

What TMS includes — explained simply

📦

GR Booking Management

Create Goods Receipt bookings in under 2 minutes. Auto-calculated freight, auto-assigned GR numbers, customer and vehicle data pre-filled from saved records. Track status from Booked to Delivered.

Market Differentiator

E-Way Bill Automation

One click from the GR screen. TMS connects directly to the government e-way bill system via a licensed GSP API. No separate portal login. No re-entering data. 20 to 60 seconds and it is done. Nobody else in this market offers this level of automation.

🖨️

Document Printing

Print GR copies, loading slips, and manifests — all formatted with your company logo and letterhead. Filter by date range or customer and print in bulk. Clean A4 format every time.

Customer Management

Save customer details once — name, mobile, GSTIN, address. Every time you create a GR, their information auto-fills. No typing the same details again and again.

🚚

Vehicle Management

Register all your vehicles with type, capacity, and owner details. Assign vehicles to bookings from a simple dropdown.

Driver Management

Save driver names, mobile numbers, and license numbers. Link drivers to bookings. Know who is carrying what at any time.

Manage your company logo, letterhead, GSTIN, and email. Download all your data anytime. Delete your account if you choose. Full control, always.
